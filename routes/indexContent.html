What is the CryptoLeague?

The CryptoLeague is a set of digital artworks that pays homage to the modern day superheroes who are building our decentralized future. Each artwork is unique and corresponds to a single figurehead in the blockchain industry. Once bought, the artwork belongs 100% to you and cannot be replicated or confiscated due to blockchain technology.
